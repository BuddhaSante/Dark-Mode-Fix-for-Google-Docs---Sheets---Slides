Dark Mode Fix for Google Docs / Sheets / Slides — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-03

This extension does not collect, sell, or transfer personal data.

What it does store:
The extension stores user preferences (for example: enabled state, per-app settings, and in-page toggle options) using Chrome extension storage on the user’s device.

What it does not do:
- Does not collect or transmit browsing history, page URLs, or analytics.
- Does not read or transmit the contents of Google Docs/Sheets/Slides documents.
- Does not sell or share data with third parties.

Changes:
If this policy changes, the updated policy will be posted at this URL.
